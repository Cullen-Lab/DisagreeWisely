* (2025-02) Proposed Resolution (June 14, 2025\)  
  * WHEREAS Simon Cullen, President of Disagree Wisely, lost employer-sponsored health coverage on May 31, 2025, and will not receive new coverage until on or about September 1, 2025;  
  * WHEREAS Disagree Wisely wishes to ensure that its President maintains continuous health coverage while continuing full-time work on the organization’s programs;  
  * RESOLVED, that, effective June 1, 2025, Disagree Wisely shall provide Simon Cullen a taxable payroll stipend equal to his monthly COBRA premium (currently $1,061.82 per month as invoiced by WEX), payable on the same cycle as regular payroll;  
  * FURTHER RESOLVED, that this stipend is treated as ordinary wages subject to all required federal, state, and local withholdings and employer taxes;  
  * FURTHER RESOLVED, that the stipend shall terminate automatically on the earlier of (a) August 31, 2025 or (b) the date Simon becomes covered by any new employer-sponsored health plan, and any excess amounts reimbursed after that date shall be repaid to the organization or offset against future compensation;  
  * FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Treasurer is authorized to make all necessary payroll entries and tax deposits to implement this resolution;  
  * FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Board finds the stipend to be reasonable and in the best interests of the organization.
