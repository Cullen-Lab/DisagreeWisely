## **AGENDA ITEM 2: INTERIM EMPLOYMENT ARRANGEMENT \- NICHOLAS DIBELLA**

### **Conflict of Interest Management**

Dr. Nicholas DiBella remained recused from discussion and voting due to direct financial interest. Dr. DiBella submitted a signed Conflict of Interest Acknowledgment prior to the meeting.

### **Discussion**

The remaining directors (Dr. Thomason and Dr. Cullen) reviewed the extraordinary circumstances: Dr. DiBella's IC Postdoctoral Research Fellowship has been indefinitely suspended due to federal funding issues beyond his control. The Board discussed:

- **Financial Impact:** Maximum cost of $31,759 for approximately 4.14 months  
- **Compensation Level:** $92,000 annually (matches current fellowship compensation)  
- **Comparable Analysis:** Significantly below market rates (25-43% lower than peer organizations)  
- **Organizational Need:** Critical for maintaining platform development and grant compliance  
- **Legal Compliance:** Meets IRC Section 4958 requirements with proper comparability data

**Documents Reviewed:**

- Board-Resolution-Interim-Employment-Nicholas-DiBella.pdf  
- Interim-Compensation-Justification-Nicholas-DiBella.pdf  
- Conflict-of-Interest-Acknowledgment-Nicholas-DiBella.pdf

### **Motion and Vote**

**Motion:** Dr. Neil Thomason moved to approve the interim employment arrangement for Dr. Nicholas DiBella as outlined in the resolution and supporting documentation.  
**Second:** Dr. Simon Cullen seconded the motion.

**Vote:**

- **In Favor:** Dr. Neil Thomason, Dr. Simon Cullen (2-0)  
- **Against:** None  
- **Abstained:** Dr. Nicholas DiBella (due to conflict of interest)  
- **Absent:** None

**Result:** MOTION CARRIED

### **Resolution and Agreement Adopted**

- Board Resolution \[2025-05\] \- Interim Employment Arrangement was duly adopted
