## Position Overview

The Chief Technology & Operations Officer (CTOO) serves as a key executive responsible for:
- Leading all technology development and architecture for the Sway platform
- Managing organizational operations including finance, compliance, and HR

This is a full-time, exempt position requiring approximately 40 hours per week.

## Justification for $100,000 Total Compensation

### Below-Market Compensation Decision

The Board has set total compensation at $100,000, which is **significantly below the identified market range** of $140,000-$165,000. This decision reflects:

1. **Fiscal Prudence**: Maximizing resources for program delivery during startup phase
2. **Mission Commitment**: Leadership's dedication to organizational sustainability
3. **Strategic Planning**: Conservative compensation allows for organizational growth

### Current Compensation Context
- **Current Position**: IC Postdoctoral Fellow, Carnegie Mellon University
- **Current Total Compensation**: $92,000 (salary plus benefits)
- **Proposed Total Compensation**: $110,000
- **Increase**: $18,000 (19.6%) despite vastly expanded executive responsibilities
