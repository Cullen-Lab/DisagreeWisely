Platform Community Guidelines

Sway by Disagree Wisely, Inc.

Effective Date: August 10, 2025

Last Updated: August 10, 2025

Purpose

Sway connects students with differing perspectives for constructive one-on-one discussions on controversial topics. These guidelines ensure conversations remain respectful and productive while preserving the intellectual freedom essential to our mission.

Core Principles

We Value:

  • Respectful Disagreement: Engaging with opposing views thoughtfully
  • Good Faith Dialogue: Approaching conversations with genuine curiosity
  • Intellectual Humility: Being open to learning from different perspectives
  • Personal Respect: Separating ideas from the people who hold them

Expected Behavior

Do:

  • Express your views clearly and respectfully
  • Listen actively to understand, not just to respond
  • Ask clarifying questions
  • Acknowledge when you learn something new
  • Disagree with ideas, not attack people
  • Use evidence and reasoning to support your points

Don't:

  • Make personal attacks or insults
  • Use slurs or derogatory language
  • Threaten or intimidate
  • Share private information about others
  • Spam or disrupt conversations
  • Misrepresent your identity or intentions

Prohibited Content

While we encourage discussion of controversial topics, the following are prohibited:

  • Harassment: Targeted attacks based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
  • Threats: Any form of threatening behavior or language
  • Doxxing: Sharing private information about other users
  • Illegal Content: Content that violates applicable laws
  • Spam: Repetitive, off-topic, or commercial content
  • Impersonation: Pretending to be someone else

Important Distinction

Disagreement ≠ Harassment

  • Challenging someone's ideas or arguments is encouraged
  • Attacking someone personally is prohibited
  • Focus on "what" is being said, not "who" is saying it

Moderation Approach

Our moderation focuses on:

  • Behavior over beliefs: We moderate conduct, not viewpoints
  • Context matters: We consider the full conversation
  • Educational intent: We aim to guide users toward constructive dialogue

Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness violations:

  1. In-Platform: Use the report feature within your conversation
  2. Email: Contact support@swaybeta.ai with:
    • Your username
    • Date/time of incident
    • Description of the issue
    • Screenshots if available

Consequences

Violations may result in:

  • Warning and guidance
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent ban from the platform

Severity of consequences depends on:

  • Nature of the violation
  • Intent and context
  • History of previous violations

Privacy and Safety

  • Conversations are private between matched participants
  • Do not share screenshots or transcripts without consent
  • Report any attempts to move conversations off-platform in early stages
  • Never share personal contact information unless you choose to

Academic Integrity

If using Sway for class assignments:

  • Follow your institution's guidelines
  • Properly attribute insights gained from conversations
  • Do not have others complete your assignments

Appeals

To appeal a moderation decision:

  • Email support@swaybeta.ai with "Appeal" in the subject line
  • Include your username and explanation
  • Appeals are reviewed within 5 business days

Updates to Guidelines

We may update these guidelines as our platform evolves. Users will be notified of significant changes.

Philosophy

These guidelines are inspired by John Stuart Mill's principle that engaging with opposing views sharpens our reasoning and deepens our understanding. We believe that respectful disagreement is not just possible but essential for intellectual growth.
