## RECUSAL COMMITMENT

To ensure compliance with applicable laws and maintain the highest standards of nonprofit governance, I hereby commit to the following:

### Complete Recusal from Decision-Making
- I will recuse myself from all Board discussions regarding my CEO appointment
- I will recuse myself from all Board discussions regarding my interim benefits package
- I will not participate in any votes related to either agenda item
- I will not attempt to influence other directors' decisions regarding these matters

### Physical Recusal During Meeting
- I will excuse myself from the meeting room (or leave the virtual meeting) during discussions of these agenda items
- I will return only after the Board has completed its deliberations and voting
- I will not listen to or observe the Board's discussions regarding these matters

### Documentation and Transparency
- I acknowledge that disinterested directors (Dr. Neil Thomason and Dr. Nicholas DiBella) will make all decisions regarding my CEO appointment
- I acknowledge that disinterested directors (Dr. Neil Thomason and Dr. Nicholas DiBella) will make all decisions regarding my benefits package
- I understand the Board's decisions, whether favorable or unfavorable, will be final and binding
- I will cooperate fully with any additional documentation or compliance requirements

### Ongoing Conflicts Management
- For my CEO appointment: Future decisions regarding my executive compensation, performance evaluation, or termination must be made by disinterested directors
- For my benefits package: Monthly payment approvals must be authorized by disinterested directors to ensure I cannot approve my own benefit payments

## LEGAL AND GOVERNANCE COMMITMENTS

### IRC 4958 Rebuttable Presumption
I understand that for these transactions to receive protection under IRC Section 4958's rebuttable presumption of reasonableness:
- Decisions must be made by disinterested directors with independent judgment
- Directors must rely on appropriate comparability data
- The decision-making process must be contemporaneously documented

My complete recusal ensures that all decisions are made by genuinely disinterested parties.

### Nonprofit Governance Best Practices
- This recusal is required for compliance with IRC Section 4958 and nonprofit governance best practices

- I will cooperate fully with any additional documentation or compliance requirements
