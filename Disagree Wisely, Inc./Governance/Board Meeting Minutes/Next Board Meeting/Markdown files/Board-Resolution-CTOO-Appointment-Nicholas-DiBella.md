# BOARD RESOLUTION
## Disagree Wisely, Inc.

**Resolution Number:** [2025-04]  
**Date:** [Meeting Date]  
**Subject:** Appointment of Dr. Nicholas DiBella as Chief Technology & Operations Officer (CTOO)

---

**WHEREAS**, Disagree Wisely, Inc. ("Organization") is a Florida nonprofit corporation seeking recognition of tax-exempt status under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code;

**WHEREAS**, Dr. Nicholas DiBella currently serves as Secretary, Treasurer, and Director of the Organization and has been instrumental in the development of the Sway platform and organizational operations;

**WHEREAS**, Dr. DiBella has demonstrated exceptional leadership in technology development, platform architecture, and operational management since the Organization's founding;

**WHEREAS**, the Organization has grown to serve thousands of students through the sophisticated Sway educational platform, requiring formal executive leadership structure;

**WHEREAS**, the Board recognizes the need to establish clear organizational leadership roles to support continued growth and external credibility;

**WHEREAS**, Dr. DiBella possesses the qualifications, experience, and demonstrated competence necessary for the Chief Technology & Operations Officer position;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED** that the Board of Directors hereby:

## Section 1: Appointment of Chief Technology & Operations Officer
Dr. Nicholas DiBella is hereby formally appointed to serve as Chief Technology & Operations Officer (CTOO) of Disagree Wisely, Inc., effective immediately upon adoption of this resolution.

## Section 2: Dual Role Authorization
Dr. DiBella shall serve concurrently as:
- Secretary & Treasurer of the Board of Directors (governance roles)
- Chief Technology & Operations Officer (executive officer role)

This dual role structure is appropriate during the Organization's current development phase and reflects Dr. DiBella's comprehensive involvement in both governance and operations.

## Section 3: Responsibilities and Authority
As CTOO, Dr. DiBella is authorized and responsible for:
- Overall technology strategy and platform development leadership
- Operations management and organizational coordination
- Financial oversight and administrative functions
- Partnership development and stakeholder relations
- Such other duties as may be assigned by the Chief Executive Officer

## Section 4: Reporting Structure
The CTOO shall report to the Chief Executive Officer and serve as a member of the Organization's executive leadership team.

## Section 5: Term of Office
This appointment shall continue indefinitely until modified by subsequent Board action or resignation of Dr. DiBella from the position.

## Section 6: Compensation
This appointment establishes the executive officer position but does not create any compensation obligations. Any future compensation arrangements shall be subject to separate Board approval following appropriate IRC Section 4958 procedures.

## Section 7: Conflict of Interest
Dr. Nicholas DiBella recused himself from all discussions and voting regarding this matter due to his direct personal interest. The resolution was approved by disinterested directors only.

---

**Against:** None  
**Abstained:** Dr. Nicholas DiBella (due to conflict of interest)
