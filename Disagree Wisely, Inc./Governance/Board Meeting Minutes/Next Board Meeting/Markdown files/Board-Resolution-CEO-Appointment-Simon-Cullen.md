# BOARD RESOLUTION
## Disagree Wisely, Inc.

**Resolution Number:** [2025-03]  
**Date:** [Meeting Date]  
**Subject:** Appointment of Chief Executive Officer

---

**WHEREAS**, Disagree Wisely, Inc. ("Organization") is a Florida nonprofit corporation seeking recognition of tax-exempt status under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code;

**WHEREAS**, the Organization requires executive leadership to manage day-to-day operations, strategic planning, fundraising, and external relations;

**WHEREAS**, Dr. Simon Cullen currently serves as President of the Board of Directors and has provided leadership during the Organization's formation and development;

**WHEREAS**, Dr. Cullen possesses the qualifications, experience, and commitment necessary to serve as Chief Executive Officer of the Organization;

**WHEREAS**, the Board desires to formalize the executive leadership structure while maintaining appropriate governance oversight;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED** that the Board of Directors hereby approves the following:

## Section 1: Appointment of Chief Executive Officer
Dr. Simon Cullen is hereby appointed as Chief Executive Officer of Disagree Wisely, Inc., effective immediately upon adoption of this resolution.

## Section 2: Dual Roles
Dr. Cullen shall serve concurrently as:
- President of the Board of Directors (governance role)
- Chief Executive Officer (executive officer role)

This dual appointment is appropriate for the Organization's current size and development stage.

## Section 3: CEO Responsibilities
As Chief Executive Officer, Dr. Cullen shall have authority and responsibility for:
- Overall strategic direction and organizational management
- Day-to-day operations and administrative oversight
- Fundraising and donor relations
- External partnerships and stakeholder engagement
- Staff management and organizational development
- Implementation of Board policies and directives

## Section 4: Reporting and Accountability
The CEO shall:
- Report regularly to the Board of Directors on organizational performance
- Provide monthly written reports on operations, finances, and strategic initiatives
- Seek Board approval for major decisions as outlined in organizational policies
- Maintain transparency in all executive activities

## Section 5: Compensation
Dr. Cullen's CEO compensation shall be addressed in a separate resolution when the Organization is financially positioned to provide executive compensation. Until such time, this appointment is made without compensation from the Organization.

## Section 6: Term and Termination
This appointment shall continue until:
- Resignation by Dr. Cullen
- Termination by majority vote of the Board of Directors
- Modification by subsequent Board resolution

## Section 7: Governance Compliance
This appointment maintains appropriate separation between governance and management functions, with the Board retaining ultimate oversight authority over organizational direction and CEO performance.
