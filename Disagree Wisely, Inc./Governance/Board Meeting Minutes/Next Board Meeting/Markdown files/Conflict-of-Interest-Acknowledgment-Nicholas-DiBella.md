## CONFLICT OF INTEREST DECLARATION

I, Dr. Nicholas DiBella, hereby acknowledge and declare the following:

### 1. Position and Interest
- I serve as Secretary, Treasurer, and Director of Disagree Wisely, Inc.
- I am the subject of both the proposed CTOO appointment and interim employment arrangement being considered by the Board
- I have direct personal and financial interests in the outcomes of the Board's decisions regarding both my executive appointment and compensation

### 2. Nature of Conflict
The conflicts of interest arise because:
- I would be the direct beneficiary of both the executive appointment and proposed compensation arrangement
- My personal and financial circumstances may influence my judgment on these matters
- My participation in the decision-making processes could compromise the independence required for proper governance

### 3. Recusal Commitment
I hereby commit to the following actions for both agenda items:
- **Complete Recusal:** I will remove myself from all Board discussions regarding these matters
- **No Participation:** I will not participate in any deliberations, questions, or comments related to my appointment or compensation
- **No Voting:** I will abstain from voting on both the CTOO appointment and interim employment agenda items
- **Physical Absence:** I will leave the meeting during consideration of these items if requested by the remaining directors

### 4. Disclosure of Circumstances
I have fully disclosed to the Board:
- The extraordinary circumstances leading to this request (suspension of IC Fellowship)
- My current financial situation and need for interim compensation
- Any other relevant facts that might influence the Board's decision

### 5. Board Independence
I acknowledge that:
- The remaining directors (Dr. Neil Thomason and Dr. Simon Cullen) have no financial interest in this decision
- They are qualified to make an independent judgment regarding the reasonableness of the proposed arrangement
- Their decision will be made based solely on the best interests of the organization

### 6. Compliance Commitment
I understand and agree that:
- This recusal is required for compliance with IRC Section 4958 and nonprofit governance best practices
- The Board's decision, whether favorable or unfavorable, will be final and binding
- I will cooperate fully with any additional documentation or compliance requirements
