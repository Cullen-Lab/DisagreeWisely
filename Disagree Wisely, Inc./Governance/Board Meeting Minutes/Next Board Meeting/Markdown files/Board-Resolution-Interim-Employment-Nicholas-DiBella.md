# BOARD RESOLUTION
## Disagree Wisely, Inc.

**Resolution Number:** [2025-05]  
**Date:** [Meeting Date]  
**Subject:** Interim Employment Arrangement for Dr. Nicholas DiBella

---

**WHEREAS**, Disagree Wisely, Inc. ("Organization") is a Florida nonprofit corporation seeking recognition of tax-exempt status under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code;

**WHEREAS**, Dr. Nicholas DiBella serves as Secretary, Treasurer, and Director of the Organization and has been instrumental in the development of the Sway platform and organizational operations;

**WHEREAS**, Dr. DiBella's Intelligence Community Postdoctoral Research Fellowship at Carnegie Mellon University has been indefinitely suspended due to federal funding issues beyond his control;

**WHEREAS**, Dr. DiBella's continued involvement in critical organizational functions including technology development, operations management, and partnership coordination is essential to the Organization's mission and ongoing commitments;

**WHEREAS**, the Board has reviewed the extraordinary circumstances necessitating this interim arrangement and finds it to be in the best interests of the Organization;

**WHEREAS**, the Board has reviewed comparable compensation data and finds the proposed compensation to be reasonable and necessary under the circumstances;

**WHEREAS**, the Board seeks to ensure compliance with all applicable laws, including Internal Revenue Code Section 4958 regarding excess benefit transactions;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED** that the Board of Directors hereby approves the following:

## Section 1: Approval of Interim Employment
The Board hereby approves an interim employment arrangement with Dr. Nicholas DiBella under the terms specified in the Interim Employment Agreement attached hereto as Exhibit A.

## Section 2: Compensation Authorization
The Board authorizes compensation of Ninety-Two Thousand Dollars ($92,000) on an annualized basis, pro-rated for the actual period of employment, which compensation the Board finds to be reasonable, necessary, and appropriate under the circumstances.

## Section 3: Payment Implementation
Compensation payments shall be processed by the Chief Executive Officer through the Organization's Gusto payroll system (linked to Bluevine business account) following completion of:
- Execution of this resolution and the employment agreement
- Establishment of myPATH account for Pennsylvania state tax withholding
- Completion of EFTPS (Electronic Federal Tax Payment System) registration
- Setup of Gusto payroll system with appropriate tax withholdings

Dr. DiBella is authorized to complete the administrative setup requirements (tax registrations and payroll system), while the Chief Executive Officer will process and authorize payments through Gusto within approximately one week of board approval. This division ensures proper conflict of interest management.

## Section 4: Employment Period
The interim employment shall commence on May 28, 2025, and shall terminate on the earlier of:
- The date Dr. DiBella's postdoctoral position resumes, or
- September 30, 2025

## Section 5: Role and Responsibilities
Dr. DiBella shall serve as Interim Technology & Operations Officer with responsibilities including but not limited to:
- Platform development and maintenance
- Operations management and coordination
- Grant administration and compliance
- Partnership and stakeholder relations
- Such other duties as may be assigned by the Chief Executive Officer

## Section 6: IRC 4958 Compliance
The Board hereby makes the following determinations required under Internal Revenue Code Section 4958:

a) **Independent Judgment:** This decision was made by disinterested directors after full consideration of the relevant facts and circumstances.

b) **Comparability Data:** The Board reviewed and considered compensation data for similar positions at comparable organizations and academic institutions.

c) **Contemporaneous Documentation:** This resolution and supporting materials constitute contemporaneous documentation of the decision-making process.

d) **Reasonableness:** The Board finds the compensation to be reasonable in light of Dr. DiBella's qualifications, the responsibilities of the position, and current market conditions.

## Section 7: Conflict of Interest
Dr. Nicholas DiBella recused himself from all discussions and voting regarding this matter due to his direct financial interest. The resolution was approved by disinterested directors only.

## Section 8: Authority to Execute
The Chief Executive Officer is hereby authorized to execute the Interim Employment Agreement and any related documents necessary to implement this resolution.

## Section 9: Extraordinary Circumstances
The Board acknowledges that this arrangement is made under extraordinary circumstances and does not constitute a precedent for future compensation decisions or employment arrangements.
