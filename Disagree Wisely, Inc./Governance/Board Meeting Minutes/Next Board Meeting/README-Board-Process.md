## Board Meeting Process

### **Meeting Setup:**
- **Format:** Virtual (Zoom/Teams/etc.) - all directors must hear each other simultaneously
- **Quorum:** All 3 directors present
- **Conflict Management:** Nicholas recuses during items 2 & 3; Simon recuses during items 1 & 4

### **Agenda Flow:**
1. **Call to Order** (Simon)
2. **Agenda Item 1: CEO Appointment (Simon Cullen)**
   - Simon recuses himself from voting
   - Discussion and voting by Neil and Nicholas (2 of 2 required)
3. **Agenda Item 2: CTOO Appointment (Nicholas DiBella)** 
   - Nicholas recuses himself from voting
   - Discussion and voting by Neil and Simon (2 of 2 required)
4. **Agenda Item 3: Interim Employment (Nicholas DiBella)** 
   - Nicholas signs conflict acknowledgment and remains recused
   - Presentation by Simon, discussion and voting by Neil and Simon (2 of 2 required)
5. **Agenda Item 4: Interim Benefits Package (Simon Cullen)**
   - Simon recuses himself from voting
   - Discussion and voting by Neil and Nicholas (2 of 2 required)
6. **Return to Full Board** (completion and next steps)

### **Required Outcome:**
- **CEO Appointment:** 2 out of 2 disinterested directors (Neil and Nicholas) must approve
- **CTOO Appointment:** 2 out of 2 disinterested directors (Neil and Simon) must approve
- **Interim Employment:** 2 out of 2 disinterested directors (Neil and Simon) must approve
- **Interim Benefits Package:** 2 out of 2 disinterested directors (Neil and Nicholas) must approve  
- **Meeting minutes** documenting all four agenda items

## PDF Conversion

### **Converting Documents to PDF for Email Distribution**

All markdown (.md) files can be automatically converted to PDF format using the included conversion script:

```bash
./auto_convert.sh
```

**Requirements:**
- The script will automatically install BasicTeX (LaTeX) if not present
- Uses pandoc with pdflatex engine for professional formatting
- All PDFs are saved to the `PDFs/` subdirectory

**What it converts:**
- Board-Resolution-CEO-Appointment-Simon-Cullen.md
- Board-Resolution-CTOO-Appointment-Nicholas-DiBella.md
- Board-Memo-Interim-Employment-Nicholas-DiBella.md  
- Board-Resolution-Interim-Employment-Nicholas-DiBella.md
- Interim-Employment-Agreement-Nicholas-DiBella.md
- Interim-Compensation-Justification-Nicholas-DiBella.md
- Board-Memo-Health-Insurance-Reimbursement-Simon-Cullen.md
- Board-Resolution-Health-Insurance-Reimbursement-Simon-Cullen.md
- CEO-Benefits-Justification-Simon-Cullen.md
- Conflict-of-Interest-Acknowledgment-Nicholas-DiBella.md

**To run the conversion:**
1. Open Terminal
2. Navigate to this directory: `cd "path/to/Board Meeting - CEO and Interim Employment"`
3. Run: `./auto_convert.sh`
4. PDFs will be created in the `PDFs/` folder and ready for email attachment

The script provides real-time feedback on conversion progress and will report success/failure for each file.
