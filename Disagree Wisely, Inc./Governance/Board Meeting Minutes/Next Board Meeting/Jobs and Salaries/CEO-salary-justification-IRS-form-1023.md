# Form 1023 Salary Justification

**Position**: Chief Executive Officer (CEO)  
**Employee**: Dr. Simon Cullen  
**Proposed Total Compensation**: $150,000 ($127,500 base + $22,500 benefits)  
**Compensation Start Date**: Year 3 (when projected revenue exceeds $1,000,000)  
**Organization**: Disagree Wisely, Inc.

## Executive Summary

The Board of Directors has set compensation for the Chief Executive Officer position following IRC Section 4958 requirements. During Years 1-2, Dr. Cullen will serve as CEO while his compensation is covered by other institutions. Beginning in Year 3, when projected revenue exceeds $1,000,000, he will transition to Disagree Wisely's payroll at the compensation level detailed below.

## Position Scope and Responsibilities

The CEO position requires 60-80 hours per week and encompasses:
- Strategic planning and organizational vision
- Leading all fundraising efforts (foundations, major donors, partnerships)
- Primary media and public representation
- Research oversight and publication
- Board relations and governance
- Partnership development with universities and educational organizations

## Comparable Organization Analysis

The Board reviewed CEO compensation data from organizations with similar missions focused on dialogue, depolarization, and educational innovation:

| Organization | 2023 Revenue | Position | Total Compensation |
|--------------|-------------|----------|-------------------|
| **Heterodox Academy** | $2.98M | Chief Executive Officer | $502,071 |
| **Braver Angels** | $5.03M | President/Executive Director | $254,753 |
| **Unify America** | $1.66M | President | $175,000 (base) |

**Revenue Context**: When CEO compensation begins in Year 3, Disagree Wisely projects revenue exceeding $1,000,000, making these organizations appropriate comparables for compensation benchmarking.

## Justification for $150,000 Compensation

- **Revenue Appropriate**: At Year 3 revenue of $1M+, $150K represents only 15% of revenue, well within nonprofit standards
- **Below Comparable CEOs**: 30-70% below CEOs at similar mission organizations ($175K-$502K range)
- **Deferred Direct Costs**: Organization saves approximately $300K by not paying CEO salary in Years 1-2
- **Conservative Approach**: Even at $1M+ revenue, compensation remains well below peer organizations
- **Workload Justified**: 60-80 hour weeks represent 1.5-2x standard full-time commitment
- **Proportional to Responsibilities**: Higher than CTOO reflecting primary leadership role, 60-80 hour weeks vs. 40 hours, and ultimate organizational accountability

## Board Approval Process

The Board followed IRC 4958 rebuttable presumption procedures:
1. Full Board approved compensation with independent director Dr. Neil Thomason and Secretary/Treasurer Dr. Nicholas DiBella voting (2 of 3 directors)
2. Obtained and reviewed current comparability data from similar nonprofits
3. Documented decision-making process in board minutes
4. Set compensation significantly below market to ensure reasonableness

## Conclusion

The $150,000 total compensation for the CEO position is reasonable and necessary, appropriately scaled from larger comparable organizations to match our revenue size while recognizing the specialized nature, extensive responsibilities, and exceptional time commitment of the role. The compensation package complies with all IRS requirements for tax-exempt organizations and demonstrates prudent fiscal management.

Supporting documentation and detailed comparability data are maintained in organizational records and available upon request.
