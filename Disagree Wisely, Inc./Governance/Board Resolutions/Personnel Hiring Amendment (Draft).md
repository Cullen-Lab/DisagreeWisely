# Amendment to Financial Authorization Board Resolution
## Disagree Wisely, Inc.
### December 13, 2025

---

**WHEREAS**, the Board of Directors adopted a Financial Authorization Board Resolution on September 28, 2025; and

**WHEREAS**, the Board wishes to delegate authority to hire non-executive employees to the President and Treasurer, acting jointly; and

**WHEREAS**, the Board wishes to ratify the hiring of Rachel Sipser as Director of Communications;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED** that the Board of Directors hereby amends the Financial Authorization Board Resolution dated September 28, 2025, as follows:

---

## A. Amendment to "Items Requiring Prior Board Approval"

The following item in the "Items Requiring Prior Board Approval" section:

> "New employees (full-time or part-time)."

Is hereby amended to read:

> "New **executive employees** (as defined below)."

---

## B. New Section: "Personnel Hiring Authority (Delegated)"

The following new section is inserted after "Expenses Allowing President or Treasurer Solo Approval":

**Personnel Hiring Authority (Delegated)**

The President and Treasurer, acting jointly, may authorize the hiring of non-executive employees, subject to the following conditions:

1. **Budget Constraint.** The position and compensation must be within the organization's approved annual budget or otherwise funded by a Board-approved grant.

2. **Employment Terms.** Employment agreements with guaranteed terms, severance provisions, or other non-standard terms require prior Board approval.

3. **Reporting.** The President or Treasurer shall report all new hires to the Board at the next regular Board meeting, including position title, start date, annual compensation, and funding source.

**Definition of Executive Employee.** For purposes of this Resolution, "executive employees" means:

- Named officers of the corporation (President, Treasurer, Secretary, or any Vice President)
- Any employee who reports directly to the Board of Directors
- Any employee whose position would constitute a "key employee" under IRS Form 990 instructions
- The Executive Director, Chief Executive Officer, Chief Operating Officer, Chief Financial Officer, Chief Technology Officer, General Counsel, or equivalent roles by any title

All other employees are "non-executive employees."

## D. Ratification of Prior Hiring

The Board hereby ratifies the hiring of **Rachel Sipser** as **Director of Communications**, effective November 16, 2025. This position is a non-executive role under the definition set forth in this amendment.

---

This amendment shall take effect upon adoption by the Board. All provisions of the September 28, 2025 Resolution not expressly amended herein remain in full force and effect.
