How Decisions Are Made

▾
  • MeetingsQuarterly via Zoom, plus special meetings as needed. May also participate by phone.
  • Email votingThe board regularly acts via unanimous written consent by email (Bylaws § 4.7 and § 8.4). A reply with your vote (including email signature) constitutes a signed consent.
  • QuorumMajority of all directors — currently 3 of 5.
  • VotingActions require a majority of directors present at a meeting with quorum. Each director has one vote.
  • Term3-year terms, staggered so roughly equal numbers expire each year.
  • Time commitmentApproximately 2 hours per month.
  • CompensationNo salary for board service. Officers may be separately compensated for operational roles (with conflict-of-interest procedures).
  • Annual meetingJanuary of each year (per Bylaws).
