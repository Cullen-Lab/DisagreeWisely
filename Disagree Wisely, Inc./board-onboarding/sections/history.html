Strategic Restructuring
Following consultation with Foundation Group, the organization adopted a clean structural separation. Sway intellectual property remains with its owners (Simon Cullen and Nick DiBella), who plan to establish a separate for-profit entity to hold it. Disagree Wisely focuses exclusively on research, education, and program delivery. This separation was recommended to avoid private benefit concerns and simplify the 501(c)(3) application. Application narrative and budget revised to reflect this structure.
